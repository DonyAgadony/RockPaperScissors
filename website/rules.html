Rules:




One Players: The game is usually played between two individuals.
but this time you will play against our advanced Ai
Three Options: Players simultaneously choose one of three options: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
Winning Combinations:
Rock beats Scissors (Rock crushes Scissors)
Scissors beats Paper (Scissors cut Paper)
Paper beats Rock (Paper covers Rock)
Draws: If both players choose the same option, the game is a tie and is typically replayed.

Fairness: The game is considered fair as each option has an equal chance of winning, making it a game of strategy and luck.
